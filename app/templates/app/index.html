Features of LawBot

LawBot

Vision for the future

Search engines like LawNet search using the individual words, leading to frustration when one does not know the exact terms to use. We imagine a system where a lawyers can simply be as specific and detailed as possible in their searches, providing as many facts as possible, and from that obtain cases which are of the same category. A search algorithm like this would first try to categories the input text using chains of catchwords, such as:
[Charities — Charity proceedings — Interpretation of s 32 of the Charities Act 1994]

From there, the algorithm will conduct a search on existing cases and their catchwords to retrieve the most relevant cases, down to the most nuanced categorisation. Young lawyers will be better equipped to find and learn about potentially esoteric categories. A classification algorithm like this will also benefit law reporting as it is automatic and systematic, leaving no room for error when tagging a case.

An AI-summarizer is beneficial on multiple levels. A brief summary of a long text is always welcome, but we envision the ability to tailor each summary given what the user wants to focus on. In a case of five issues, perhaps one may just want a summary of only one of them. Tailored summaries using AI greatly increase the efficieny of research and allow the lawyers to craft precise arguments. Headnotes for law reports can thus be automatically generated using this technology.

The culmination of all these ideas lies in a chatbot, one that is connected to our legal database, ensuring its ability to retrieve relevant local cases, and back up its statements with pinpoint citations. With additional training on legal data, it will help even the most experienced lawyer formulate arguments and test strategies in prolonged conversation.

Research Assistant
A lawyer can interact with LawBot to get help during research

The AI bot will act like a lawyer and answer every question professionally. You can even have a prolonged discussion with it


Example input :

What is a tripartite agreement?

What happens if a guarantor doesnt sign?

Are there any cases on this?

Legal Taxonomizer
Our server can classify any text into different legal categories

By gathering more than 5,000 cases from elitigation.sg, we are able to use a machine learning algorithm to predict the class of cases given any text input. Cases with similar tags will be retrieved and shown, along with an AI-generated summary
